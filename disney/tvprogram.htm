TV program

"Dit is Disney" (This is Disney) was broadcasted on the Dutch TV from 1985 till 1989. It was a program of the NCRV, one of the Dutch public broadcasters. "Dit is Disney" consisted of a selection of old Disney shorts, Disney funfacts, news from the themeparks and the newer Disney series like Ducktales. Not only did they show the more famous and popular Mickey Mouse and Donald Duck shorts, but also old Alice and Silly Symphonies cartoons. And every week they had a videoclip of a pop-song, like "Dancing in the Streets" and "Baby Love", which was made out of fragments of Disney cartoons.
